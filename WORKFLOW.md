# Generic Vendor Workflow

```text
Add vendor section to links.md
        ↓
Run:
/track-vendor <VendorName>
        ↓
Generate:
drafts/<vendor>.md
        ↓
Run:
/generate-report
        ↓
Vendor automatically appears in models.md
```

`/track-all` also scans links.md for additional vendors automatically.
